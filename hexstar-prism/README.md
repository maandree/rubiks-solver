# Additional notation

* [↑], [↓], and [↕] conbines ← and →.

* [↖] and [↘] conbines ↗ and ↙.

* [↗] and [↙] conbines ↖ and ↘.

* [⤸¹] rotates the whole cube 60° clockwise.

* [⤸²] rotates the whole cube 120° clockwise.

* [⤸³] rotates the whole cube 180°.

* [⤹²] rotates the whole cube 120° anticlockwise.

* [⤹¹] rotates the whole cube 60° anticlockwise.
